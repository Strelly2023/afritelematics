    Canonical constitutional definition of admissibility within

    replay artifact, transcript, receipt, mutation, or runtime
    action may constitutionally participate in:
      - runtime execution
      - replay verification
      - governance evaluation
      - constitutional enforcement
      - proof generation

    Admissibility is a constitutional validation property.
    It is not equivalent to:
      - implementation existence
      - filesystem presence
      - execution success
      - operational liveness
