# AfriTech Replay Verification Reports

This directory contains **immutable replay verification artifacts**
generated by the AfriTech constitutional replay verifier.

These reports provide durable, machine-readable evidence that the
current system state is derivable lawfully from its constitutional
history.

---

## Authority

Replay reports are:

- ❌ NON-AUTHORITATIVE
- ❌ NON-EXECUTABLE
- ✅ AUDITABLE
- ✅ IMMUTABLE

Authority remains exclusively with:
- the registry
- epoch history
- ADRs

---

## When Reports Are Generated

Replay reports MAY be generated:
- during CI execution
- during audit preparation
- during external review
- after major governance milestones

Replay reports MUST NOT be required for runtime execution.

---

## Immutability Rules

Once written:
- reports MUST NOT be modified
- reports MUST NOT be deleted silently
- new reports MUST be written for new replay runs

Historical correctness requires preservation, not replacement.

---

## Verification Model

Each report contains:
- verified epoch range
- terminal epoch
- registry alignment state
- ADR legitimacy checks
- sealing and invariant confirmations
- cryptographic epoch hash chain

Reports are fully self-describing and can be validated
independently using the published schema.
