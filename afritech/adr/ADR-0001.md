# 📜 ADR‑0001 — Constitutional Activation and Sovereign Authority Establishment

## Status
**ACCEPTED (FOUNDATIONAL)**

## Date
**2026‑05‑01**

## Scope
**GLOBAL — SYSTEM CONSTITUTION**

---

## 1. Context

AfriTech has evolved from an architecturally disciplined system into a formally defined **constitutional system** with explicit governance, immutability, and lineage constraints.

Prior to this decision:

- The system existed in multiple implementation lineages (`afritech_v1`, `afritech`)
- Architectural rules, dependency laws, and invariants were defined
- Kernel, registry, and proof structures were present
- Runtime execution was possible, but ultimate authority boundaries were transitional

A single, explicit, and irreversible act was required to:

- Establish a **sole sovereign authority**
- Preserve historical lineage without permitting execution
- Activate the registry as the **ultimate source of truth**
- Define the **only lawful mechanism for evolution**

This ADR constitutes that **foundational constitutional act**.

---

## 2. Decision

We hereby declare and ratify the following decisions, which together form the binding constitution of AfriTech.

### 2.1 Sovereign Authority

- The namespace **`/afritech`** is established as the **sole sovereign authority root** of the AfriTech system.
- All execution, governance, validation, and legitimacy derive exclusively from this root.
- No other namespace, module, lineage, or historical artifact may claim parallel or independent authority.

### 2.2 Registry as Source of Truth

- `afritech/registry/registry.yaml` is activated as the **constitutional source of truth**.
- The registry exclusively defines:
  - authority and legitimacy
  - invariants and enforcement requirements
  - epoch state and constitutional history
  - permitted and forbidden mutation procedures
- All runtime behavior, CI enforcement, and execution legitimacy are strictly subordinate to the registry.

### 2.3 Historical Lineage Preservation

- The predecessor implementation **`afritech_v1`** is frozen as **historical lineage**.
- The predecessor:
  - retains audit, reference, and reconstruction value
  - **does not** retain execution rights or sovereign authority
- No code, state, or artifact from `afritech_v1` may be executed, mutated, or interpreted as authoritative.

### 2.4 Kernel and Formal Authority

- The formal kernel (Lean layer and its governed surfaces) defines the **immutable structural constraints** of the system.
- Kernel immutability is mandatory and enforceable.
- Kernel identity is constitutionally significant and subject to cryptographic verification via the registry.

### 2.5 Mutation and Evolution

- All mutation is **forbidden by default**.
- Lawful change requires **all** of the following, in strict order:
  1. An approved Architectural Decision Record (ADR)
  2. Explicit epoch advancement
  3. Registry resealing
- Silent mutation, rollback, unilateral override, or tool‑based bypass is constitutionally forbidden.

---

## 3. Effects

This decision has the following immediate and irreversible effects:

- Constitutional sovereignty is established
- Dual‑sovereign ambiguity is eliminated
- Registry authority is activated and supreme
- Historical predecessor is frozen without erasure
- System boot becomes a **constitutional act**
- Future change becomes jurisdictional, not discretionary

This ADR consumes the **foundational authority slot** and must never be superseded, reused, or reinterpreted by later decisions.

---

## 4. Epoch Assignment

- This decision establishes **Epoch 0**
- All future epochs are strict, monotonic successors of this act
- No epoch may precede, bypass, or reinterpret this decision

---

## 5. Non‑Goals

This ADR explicitly does **not**:

- Introduce new features
- Modify business or application logic
- Validate new proofs, schemas, or implementations
- Advance system evolution beyond authority establishment

All such actions are deferred to subsequent ADRs under this constitution.

---

## 6. Approval

This ADR is approved by virtue of:

- Successful sovereign boot under `/afritech`
- Registry activation as source of truth
- Freezing of predecessor lineage
- Enforcement of constitutional guards at runtime and in CI

No external, discretionary, or additional approval mechanism exists or is required.

---

## 7. Final Assertion

This ADR constitutes the **foundational constitution of AfriTech**.

All subsequent decisions, epochs, implementations, and evolutions derive legitimacy **only insofar as they are consistent with this act**.

Any action inconsistent with this ADR is **constitutionally invalid**, regardless of intent, tooling, or execution context.
