# Proof Surfaces

AfriRide’s guarantees are expressed through **explicit proof surfaces**.
Each proof surface is a constitutionally bounded, replay‑verifiable
demonstration of deterministic coordination under defined conditions.

No proof surface replaces a prior one.
Each extension is **additive, auditable, and governed**.

---

## AfriRide v0.1 — Baseline Proof Surface  
**Epoch:** 6  
**Status:** FROZEN (Immutable)

AfriRide v0.1 establishes the foundational Proof‑of‑Continuity.

### Proven Properties
- Deterministic coordination under driver degradation
- Explicit authority enforcement
- Canonical decision trace generation
- Replay hash equality

### Verified Outcome
- **Decision Determinism Rate (DDR): 1.0**

### Governance Meaning
AfriRide v0.1 serves as the **immutable reference baseline**.
Its artifacts, claims, and evidence may never be modified,
reinterpreted, or weakened by future evolution.

---

## AfriRide v0.2 — Expanded Proof Surface  
**Epoch:** 7  
**Status:** FROZEN (Immutable)

AfriRide v0.2 extends the proof surface to a **bounded, explicitly declared failure space**,
while preserving all guarantees established in v0.1.

### Declared Failure Space
The following failure scenarios are exhaustively defined and governed:

- `DRIVER_DROPOUT`  
- `DRIVER_REJECTION_CHAIN`  
- `TIMEOUT_EXCEEDED`  

No other failure modes are permitted within v0.2.

---

### Proven Properties (Across All Scenarios)
- Deterministic decision causality
- Replay‑verifiable execution
- Explicit deterministic refusal on exhaustion or timeout
- Identical decision traces under replay

### Verified Outcomes
- **Decision Determinism Rate (DDR): 1.0** (all scenarios)
- **Continuity Coverage (CC): 1.0**
- **Deterministic Refusal Rate (DRR): 1.0**

Any deviation from these outcomes would invalidate the proof surface.

---

## Relationship Between Proof Surfaces

- AfriRide v0.1 remains permanently authoritative
- AfriRide v0.2 does not alter or reinterpret v0.1
- v0.2 exists as a lawful extension under a new epoch
- Both proof surfaces coexist and are independently auditable

This layered structure demonstrates that **proof can scale without historical mutation**.

---

## Summary

AfriRide’s proof surfaces collectively demonstrate that:

> **Deterministic, authority‑governed coordination can remain replay‑verifiable across increasing failure complexity, without sacrificing correctness or governance integrity.**

Each proof surface is executable.
Each claim is bounded.
Each guarantee is enforced by replay.
