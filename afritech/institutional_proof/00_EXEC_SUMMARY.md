# AfriRide — Proof‑of‑Continuity (Institutional Summary)

AfriRide is **not a product**.  
It is a **constitutionally governed Proof‑of‑Continuity system** demonstrating that **deterministic, authority‑correct coordination** remains **replay‑verifiable** under degraded operating conditions.

The system exists to answer a single institutional question:

> **Can coordinated decision‑making remain correct, auditable, and identical under failure, without relying on optimization, heuristics, or probabilistic behavior?**

AfriRide answers **yes**, with executable evidence.

---

## Proof Surfaces

AfriRide provides two constitutionally sealed proof surfaces:

### • AfriRide v0.1 — Baseline Proof (Epoch 6, Frozen)
- Demonstrates deterministic coordination under a single degraded‑input scenario
- Produces canonical decision traces
- Guarantees replay hash equality
- Serves as the immutable reference baseline

### • AfriRide v0.2 — Expanded Proof (Epoch 7, Frozen)
- Extends the proof to a **bounded, explicitly declared failure space**
- Covers multiple deterministic failure scenarios:
  - driver dropout
  - rejection chains
  - coordination timeout
- Preserves all v0.1 guarantees without modification
- Demonstrates continuity across scenarios with identical replay outcomes
