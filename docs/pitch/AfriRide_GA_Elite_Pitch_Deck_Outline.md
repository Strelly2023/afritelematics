# AfriRide GA Elite Pitch Deck Outline

STATUS: INVESTOR DECK OUTLINE  
CLASSIFICATION: INVESTOR COMMUNICATION SURFACE  

## 1. Problem

African mobility is fragmented, informal, and weakly verified. Riders, drivers,
businesses, fleets, and regulators lack a shared source of operational truth.

## 2. Market Opportunity

Mobility, digital payments, fleet operations, business transport, and city
infrastructure are converging. Africa needs infrastructure that supports both
consumer mobility and institutional trust.

## 3. Solution

AfriRide is a replay-verified mobility platform where rides, routes, fares,
payments, earnings, and compliance reports can be reconstructed from evidence.

## 4. Product

- Rider app
- Driver app
- Operator dashboard
- Fleet and business account tools
- Replay receipts
- Regulatory reporting layer

## 5. Technology Advantage

- EventLog-backed truth
- GPS evidence chain
- route replay
- replay-backed payments
- trust/fraud review from evidence
- compliance-ready report hashes

## 6. Business Model

- ride commission
- driver subscriptions
- fleet SaaS
- business account billing
- payment processing margin
- regulatory reporting services
- city/operator data insights

## 7. Go-To-Market

- controlled MVP pilot
- fleet/business proof pilots
- payment validation
- multi-country expansion
- regulatory and public transport partnerships

## 8. Competitive Advantage

```text
Competitors optimize rides.
AfriRide verifies rides.
```

AfriRide competes on trust infrastructure, not only rider/driver UX.

## 9. Roadmap

1. MVP Pilot
2. Commercial Launch
3. Multi-Country Expansion
4. Enterprise Platform
5. Infrastructure Layer

## 10. Financial Model

Use ride volume, take rate, payment margin, subscriptions, fleet SaaS accounts,
and business ride volume as core model drivers.

## 11. Team

Founder-led execution with technical, operations, compliance, and market-entry
roles to be filled as the pilot proves.

## 12. Investment Ask

Raise to convert GA Elite architecture into field-proven MVP traction.

## Status Boundary

This deck must not claim production readiness, regulatory approval, government
contracts, real payment volume, or market traction until evidence exists.
