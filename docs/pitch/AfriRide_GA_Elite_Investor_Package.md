## 3. Problem

African mobility systems are fragmented:

- informal transport creates uneven reliability
- dispatch is often opaque or weakly coordinated
- payment integration is inconsistent across countries
- fleet owners lack verified operational visibility
- business mobility lacks replayable billing evidence
- regulators receive incomplete or delayed reporting
- trust between riders, drivers, operators, and governments is fragile

The deeper problem:

```text
Mobility platforms optimize rides,
but most cannot prove the full truth of what happened.
```

## 4. Solution

AfriRide provides a replay-verified mobility platform where every ride, route,

reconstructed from evidence.
