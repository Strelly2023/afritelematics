Purpose: present AfriRide as replay-backed trust infrastructure for mobility,

or guaranteed market capture.

1. category: trust infrastructure for mobility
2. problem: fragmented trust across riders, drivers, operators, partners, and governments
3. system: trace -> replay -> evidence -> receipt -> anchor -> registry -> network
4. partner platform: APIs, SDK, onboarding, tutorials
5. enterprise hardening: audit bundles, legal-proof formats, compliance alignment
6. advanced cryptography: Merkle batching, zk attestation, multi-party verification
7. trust network architecture: publishers, registry, verifiers, observers
8. go-to-market: enterprise pilots, audit integrations, government pathways
9. launch proof boundary: what is proven, what remains bounded
10. raise and partnership ask
