# AfriRide First Partner Pitch Script And Deck

Status: FIRST PARTNER PITCH SCRIPT AND DECK
Classification: MEETING_READY_PARTNER_COMMERCIAL_SURFACE

Purpose: provide a meeting-ready script and deck structure for the first
partner pitch conversation.

This document is a communication and sales surface.
It is not proof that a partner contract is already signed.

## Meeting Goal

Leave the first partner meeting with one of these outcomes:

1. agreement to a sandbox verification session
2. agreement to a bounded pilot scope
3. agreement to a commercial follow-up on the first contract offer

## 30-Minute Meeting Flow

### Minute 0-3. Opening Script

Use this opening:

```text
We are not asking you to trust another dashboard.
We are showing you a way to verify operational truth for disputed rides,
audit workflows, and partner review without exposing your internal systems or
asking you to accept unverifiable claims.
```

### Minute 3-8. Problem Framing

Say:

```text
Most mobility and fleet workflows break down when a ride is disputed, when an
operator needs audit evidence, or when a partner needs proof without direct
database access. AfriRide solves that by turning trace, replay, receipt, and
verification into one bounded trust workflow.
```

### Minute 8-14. Product Demonstration

Show:

- Trust Explorer Registry
- Verification Visualization
- one verified packet
- one rejected packet diagnosis
- one replay-linked authority boundary note

### Minute 14-20. Workflow Value

Tie the demo to one partner workflow:

- dispute handling
- fleet exception handling
- audit packet generation
- public-interest visibility

Use this line:

```text
The value is not another operational surface. The value is that your team can
explain what happened, prove what happened, and package what happened without
guesswork.
```

### Minute 20-24. Commercial Proposal

Say:

```text
We recommend starting with one bounded pilot, one workflow, one owner, one
success metric, and one fixed commercial package rather than a broad platform
replacement.
```

### Minute 24-28. Risk And Objection Handling

Be ready to answer:

- why not just use logs
- why this is not too complex
- why this does not require replacing existing systems
- how compliance and public-sector review fit

### Minute 28-30. Close

Close with:

```text
If this is valuable, the next step is a sandbox verification session this week
and a pilot scoping call immediately after. We will bring the exact workflow,
success metrics, and first contract proposal.
```

## Deck Structure

1. title: replay-backed trust infrastructure for partner workflows
2. problem: unverifiable disputes, weak audit evidence, slow exception handling
3. solution: trace -> replay -> receipt -> registry -> verification
4. demo: Trust Explorer and one packet walkthrough
5. workflow value: where this saves time and reduces risk
6. pilot scope: one workflow, one owner, one metric, one timeline
7. commercial offer: first contract pricing
8. authority boundary: what AfriRide proves and what it does not claim
9. next step: sandbox session and pilot proposal
