Before the Session

  • Confirm your assigned ID: R1, R2, or D1, D2.
  • Open the app once and confirm it connects to the AfriRide test backend.
  • Keep normal mobile data enabled unless an observer asks for a specific network test.

During the Session

  • Use the app naturally.
  • Do not reinstall, log out, switch devices, or manually clear app data.
  • Report visible issues to the observer; do not attempt to repair the run.

Test Classification

Installability is execution-enabling only. Operational legitimacy requires field execution, complete trace capture, replay verification, and post-run validation.
